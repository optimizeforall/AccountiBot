Hello, I'm AccountBot.

To add a new goal:
!g [goal duration] [hours to work] [goal title] ?[rest days]

Examples: 
!g 10 30 Learn_Rust 
!g 10 30 Learn_Rust 3

To add hours to goal
!ga [goal_title] [time in hours / minutes]

Examples:
!ga Learn_Rust 1h55m
!ga Learn_Rust 90m

To show graph of progress:
!gg [goal title]

Example:
!gg Learn_Rust
